# Requirements Document

## Project Title
AI Digital Twin Platform for Predictive Rural Development in Bharat

## Problem Statement
Rural India faces challenges in agriculture, water management, employment planning, and disaster preparedness due to lack of predictive AI systems and integrated data platforms.

## Objectives
- Provide AI-based predictive insights for agriculture and climate.
- Improve water resource planning.
- Support rural employment and migration analysis.
- Enable proactive disaster risk management.

## Functional Requirements
- Real-time data ingestion from weather, satellite, and government datasets.
- AI predictive analytics engine.
- Multilingual user dashboard.
- Analytics reporting system.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Scalable cloud infrastructure.
- Secure data handling.
- High availability and performance.
